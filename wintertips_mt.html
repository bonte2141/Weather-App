Winter Tips and Tricks

Home Winterizing

Insulate your home to retain heat, prevent pipes from freezing, and check your heating system.

Outdoor Safety

Wear layers, know the signs of frostbite and hypothermia, and carry a winter emergency kit.

Winter Driving

Install winter tires, keep a car emergency kit, and learn to navigate icy roads safely.

Health Tips

Stay active, eat healthily, and ensure you get enough Vitamin D during the shorter daylight hours.

Energy Efficiency

Keep your energy bills down by using draft stoppers on doors and windows, setting your thermostat to a lower temperature at night, and using a programmable thermostat to manage your heating efficiently.

Snow Removal

Stay ahead of the snow by regularly clearing your driveways and walkways. Use pet-safe deicing substances and ensure you have the proper equipment like shovels and snow blowers ready and in good working order.

Prepare for Power Outages

Winter storms can often lead to power outages. Be prepared with a home generator, battery backups, flashlights, and a supply of non-perishable food and water.

Indoor Air Quality

With homes sealed tight against the cold, indoor air quality can suffer. Use humidifiers to maintain comfortable humidity levels, ensure your home is well-ventilated, and replace HVAC filters regularly. Consider indoor plants to help clean the air.
