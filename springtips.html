Spring Tips and Tricks

Allergy Management

Spring brings pollen, so it's crucial to manage allergies. Keep windows closed on high-pollen days and use an air purifier to maintain a pollen-free indoor environment.

Spring Cleaning

It's the perfect time to declutter and clean your home. Consider donating items you no longer need and refreshing your space.

Garden Prep

Prepare your garden by clearing out weeds, testing soil, and starting seedlings indoors if it's still cool outside.

Home Maintenance

Inspect your home for any damage caused by winter and make necessary repairs. Clean gutters and downspouts to prevent water damage.

Outdoor Safety

As outdoor activities increase, remember to apply sunscreen, stay hydrated, and wear appropriate gear for your adventures.

Pest Control

With warmer weather, pests become more active. Seal any entry points in your home and keep your yard clear of debris.

Energy Efficiency

As the days get warmer, look to conserve energy. Use curtains to keep out heat during the day and reduce air conditioning use.

Prepare for Rain

Spring can be rainy, so ensure you have good drainage around your home. Check your sump pump if you have one and consider a rain barrel to collect water for gardening.
