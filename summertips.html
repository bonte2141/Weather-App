Summer Tips and Tricks

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to prevent dehydration, especially if you're active or spending time outside.

Sun Protection

Use sunscreen with at least SPF 30, wear hats, and seek shade during the midday hours when the sun is strongest.

Cooling Your Home

Use fans and air conditioning efficiently. Close blinds during the day to block the sun and open windows at night if it's cooler outside.

Outdoor Activities

Plan your outdoor activities in the early morning or late evening to avoid the hottest parts of the day.

Summer Gardening

Water your garden early in the morning to prevent evaporation and choose drought-resistant plants to conserve water.

Food Safety

Be careful when cooking outdoors. Keep perishable foods in the cooler and ensure meats are cooked to the proper temperatures to prevent foodborne illnesses.

Pest Prevention

Keep mosquitoes at bay by eliminating standing water where they breed and using insect repellent when outdoors.

Prepare for Summer Storms

Check the weather forecast regularly, secure loose outdoor items, and have a plan in case of severe weather.
