Autumn Tips and Tricks

Layered Clothing

The weather can change quickly in autumn, so dressing in layers allows for easy adjustments throughout the day.

Home Heating Maintenance

Before the cold weather arrives in full force, have your heating system checked and serviced by a professional to ensure it's ready for winter.

Leaf Management

Regularly rake leaves and dispose of them properly to prevent them from blocking drainage systems and creating slippery surfaces.

Prepare Your Car for Winter

Check antifreeze levels, tire tread depth, and consider switching to winter tires if you live in a snowy area.

Seasonal Allergies

Keep windows closed on high pollen days and use air purifiers to manage indoor air quality as plants release pollen before winter.

Check Smoke Detectors and Carbon Monoxide Detectors

Ensure they are working properly and replace batteries if necessary as you begin to use your heating system more often.

Fall Gardening

Plant spring bulbs, winterize your garden, and prune back summer growth to prepare your garden for the colder months.

Holiday Preparations

Begin planning for holiday activities early to reduce stress and ensure you have all necessary supplies.
